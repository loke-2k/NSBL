As you navigate your basketball journey, you'll encounter two primary currencies: SP and XP. Each fuels a different aspect of your player's growth.

**Our Currencies**

* **SP:**  This currency is used to upgrade attributes and badges.
* **XP:**  This currency is used to upgrade tendencies and buy modifications (animations, signature moves, etc).

**Earning Currency**

Our league prioritizes rich narratives. Engaging media and creative storylines are rewarded with SP, fueling your player's growth. Remember, greatness takes time –  patience and dedication are key!

## Teams & Contracts

**Salary Cap**

Our league operates with a clear salary cap of $1500.

> Teams who are at $1500 or below during the season can take on upto $1600 in contracts through trades during the season.

**Contract Tiers**

* **$150–$300:** All players are eligible.
* **$300–$350:** Must have played one season.
* **$350–$400:** Must have played two seasons.
* **$400–$500:** Must have played two seasons and been awarded with an All-Star or All-TRC 2nd award.
* **$500–$600:** Must have played two seasons and been awarded a MVP, DPOY, or All-TRC 1st award.

**Contract Clauses**

* **No Trade Clause:** Exclusive to supermax contracts, restricting trades without player consent.
* **No Cut Clause:**  No limitations; any player can be offered a contract with this clause.

**Contract Options**

These clauses can be negotiated during new contracts or extensions.

**Contract Splits**

Contracts are divided between SP and XP.

| Player | Amount | SP (30%) | XP (70%) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Player 1 | $600 | 180SP | 420XP |


## Drafted Rookie Contracts
Rookie contracts don't hit your cap as hard as regular ones do.

- **💵 Cap Hit:** $150
- **💸 Their Salary:** $240

> Rookies will earn $240 each time they claim; they'll only count as $150 towards your salary cap.
