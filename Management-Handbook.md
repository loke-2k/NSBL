# **Team Management Handbook**

![Markelle Irving shooting a jumpshot.](https://i.imgur.com/K6d6jEM.png)

Managing a successful team is a challenging yet rewarding journey. Follow this handbook to hone your skills and guide your team to the championship.

---

## **Logs**

- **Submission Deadline:** Team logs are due by **12PM EST** on game day. Any changes to this deadline will be communicated via staff announcements.
- **Late Submissions:** Logs turned in after the deadline will remain unchanged until the next week.
> You may not use the '13 Heat or '08 Rockets playbook.

---

## **Trades**

### **Trade Approval**
- All trades must receive **administrator approval**.
- Approval is based on the rationale behind the trade, not just its balance.
> Teams under new management are not shielded from making poor trade decisions.

### **Trade Restrictions**
- **Player Trades:** A player must play a minimum of **three games** with their current team before being eligible for trade.
- **Draft Picks:** Tradeable only if the pick is not more than **two years in advance**.

### **Trading Over The Cap**
- Teams can trade up to **$1,600** of their total salary cap during the season.
- **Important:** If your team is over the **$1,500 salary cap**, you cannot execute a trade unless it brings your cap under $1,500.

---

## **Contracts**

### **Contract Clauses**

#### **No-Trade Clause (NTC)**
- Must be exercised within **24 hours** of the trade posting in the pending trades channel.

#### **No-Cut Clause (NCC)**
- A player with an NCC must request their own release with team approval.
- For inactive players, a release requires **administrator approval**.

### **Releasing Contracts**
- **Active Players:** Require both the player’s agreement and administrator approval. Note that active players making above the minimum cannot be released in the first season.
- **Inactive Players:** Can be released with administrator approval.
- **NCC Players:** Release necessitates approval from both the player and the administrator.

### **Signing Surge Players**
- Surge players are available on a **first-come-first-serve** basis (excluding rookies and stashes).
- They sign for a minimum one-year deal at **$150**.
- Each surge team may veto one call-up request per season. Additionally, if a veto is exercised, that team is barred from making another call-up request for the same player during that season.

### **Signing Free Agents**

#### **In-Season Free Agency**
- Teams may **claim** players at the minimum contract amount ($150) following the waiver order.
- Note: Rookies are ineligible as they must complete a mandatory season in the Surge league.

#### **Restructuring Contracts**
- Each team can restructure one contract during the off-season. 
- **Restructure Definition:** This is considered a completely new contract agreement.

---

## **Blow It Up!**

Once every five seasons, a team can opt to use the **Blow It Up!** feature, which clears the entire roster and places all players on the free agent market. Eligibility requires:

- A **losing record** in the most recent season.
- **Missing the playoffs** in the last two consecutive seasons.
