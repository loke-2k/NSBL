# Season Flow
Our league has simple standards.

## ⚙ Settings
- Eighteen games; no conferences.
- Five games series-setup in the playoffs.
- Six total playoff teams with seeds 5 and 6 decided by the **Play-In Tournament.**

## 📅 Deadlines

- All-Star Weekend after **Week 13**.
- All deadlines (trade, signing, call-up) after **Week 13**.

## 🤔 What is the Play-In Tournament?

1. **Five Seed Game:**
    - **Five Seed** versus the **Six Seed**.
    - Winner receives the **Five Seed.**
    - Loser has another chance in the **Six Seed** game.

2. **Six Seed Game - Qualifier:** 
    - **Seven Seed** versus the **Eight Seed**.
    - Winner moves on to the **Six Seed Game** (playing the loser of the **Five Seed Game**).
    - Loser is eliminated from playoff contention.

4. **Six Seed Game:**
    - Loser of the **Five Seed Game** versus the winner of the **Six Seed Game - Qualifier.**
    - Winner receives the **Six Seed.**
    - Loser is eliminated from playoff contention.

## 🎮 Game Settings
- **Quarter Length:**
    - 7 minutes (Main League)
    - 5 minutes (Surge League)
- **Freelance Series Order:** Static
- **Freelance Series Repeat:** Do Not Repeat
- **Adapative Coaching Engine:** Off
- **Fatigue:** Off
- **Injuries:** Off
