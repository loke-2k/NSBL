# **Manager Points System**

![A basketball player animation.](https://i.imgur.com/IXGrvVh.png)

Manager Points (MP) serve as a **performance evaluation system** for team managers. By making strategic moves, achieving success on the court, and maintaining strong decision-making, managers accumulate points. At the end of each season, their status is re-evaluated based on these points, determining whether they retain their position.

---

## **How Manager Points Work**

### **Team Performance Metrics**
Success on the court directly affects a manager's MP. Each game and postseason achievement contributes to a manager’s overall score.

| Event | Impact on MP |
|-------|------------|
| **Win (W)** | +4 points per win |
| **Loss (L)** | -2 points per loss |
| **Playoff Appearance (PA)** | +10 points |
| **Finals Appearance (FA)** | +10 points |
| **Championship Win (CW)** | +25 points |
| **Losing Season (LS)** *(negative win-loss record)* | -10 points |
| **Swept in Playoffs (SP)** *(lost series 0-3)* | -5 points |
| **Missed Playoffs (MPL)** | -10 points |

---

## **Evaluation & Job Security**
At the end of each season, a manager’s total MP is assessed.

- **Managers falling at or below 10 MP will be dismissed** by their team’s ownership.
- If a manager has a losing season but maintains 50+ MP, their points will be reset to 50, then evaluated.
- Each championship win adds +25 MP to the 50 MP reset value, allowing long-term success to impact future evaluations. For example:
    - 1 championship → reset to 75 MP
    - 2 championships → reset to 100 MP
    - 3 championships → reset to 125 MP

---

## **Ownership Influence on Manager Points**
Each team has an **owner** with unique priorities. Meeting or failing to meet these priorities affects MP by **2-10 points**, depending on the weight of the category.

### **Revenue Star System**
Revenue is a key factor in managerial success. Each team’s revenue rating (1 to 5 stars) **modifies MP impact** as follows:

| Revenue Rating | MP Impact |
|---------------|----------|
| **1 Star**  | ±2 points |
| **2 Stars** | ±4 points |
| **3 Stars** | ±6 points (double impact) |
| **4 Stars** | ±8 points |
| **5 Stars** | ±10 points (maximum impact) |

For example, a **manager with 3 stars in revenue** will gain or lose **6 MP** based on their revenue performance. A **5-star manager** could see a swing of **10 MP**, making financial success a crucial aspect of team management.

> Revenue is just one of the many star systems. However, they all follow this same system.

---

## **Why This System Matters**
- **Data-Driven Accountability:** Provides a structured way to assess managerial success.
- **Encourages Competitive Teams:** Rewards good team-building and strategic planning.
- **Promotes Active Engagement:** Keeps managers invested in both on-court and off-court decisions.
- **Balanced Job Security:** Gives managers room to recover from setbacks while rewarding long-term success.

By integrating performance, decision-making, and financial success, this system ensures that **only the best managers stay at the helm** of their teams!
