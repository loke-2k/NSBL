## 💻 Create Player
Go to our website by clicking [here](https://rcup.live/) and make sure you have an account before you start this tutorial.

* **🖐 Player Choice:** Choose your first name, last name, position, college background, and country. However... height, weight, and wingspan are random based on your player's position.
* **🐤 Starting Upgrades:** Every player starts with a neutral base – all attributes sit at a solid 55, and badges are yet to be earned.
* **❌ No Caps:** There's no attribute, badge, or tendency limit.

## 🎲 Random Aspects
Leave some aspects to fate! These player characteristics are randomly generated.

* **Height:** Heights are determined by position.
* **Weight:** Similar to height, weight is influenced by position.
* **Wingspan:** Wingspans are randomly assigned within a broad range (40-100).
* **Jumpshot:** Based on your player's height, a jump shot animation is automatically assigned. Don't worry, you can personalize this later with earned XP!

## 📏 Important Physicals

Let's delve into the physical aspects that define your player.

* **Height:** Taller heights lower the speed, agility, and vertical attributes.
* **Weight:** Weight is purely cosmetic. 
* **Strength:** Strength is randomly generated based on position.

## 💎 Anomalies

### Benefits

* **Growth Spurt:** A random height boost between 1 to 3 inches.
* **Extra Cash:**  Anomalies start with a 30% increase in starting currency.
* **Physical Boost:** Physical attributes will be increased randomly by 5 to 10 points.

> Each player has a **1.5%** chance to transform into an anomaly upon creation.

## 🚫 Height Limits

- **1️⃣ PG:** 6'5
- **2️⃣ SG:** 6'7
- **3️⃣ SF:** 6'9
- **4️⃣ PF:** 6'11

> These are not the maximum heights for player creations. Instead, they provide a guideline for out-of-position players who are trying to play a different position.

## 🟩 Mandatory Surge Year

All new players will be required to play a mandatory year in the Surge before joining a team. 

> This policy ensures that these players develop their skills and are fully prepared for the competitive intensity of our league.
